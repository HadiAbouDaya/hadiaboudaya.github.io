## Image Format Guidelines

| Use case | Format | Max size |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Photos | `.jpg` or `.webp` | < 300KB after compression |
| Logos & icons | `.svg` or `.png` (transparent) | < 50KB |
| Badges | `.png` | < 100KB |
| OG previews | `.jpg` | 1200x630, < 200KB |
| Favicon | `.ico` + `.svg` | Standard sizes |
| Blog covers | `.jpg` or `.webp` | 1200x675, < 250KB |

Use [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app) or [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com) to compress before adding.
